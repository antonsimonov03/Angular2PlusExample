How It Works?

1

List Your Car

Create your advert in less than 60 seconds! Our platform does all the heavy lifting for you by populating the features of your car and suggest an ideal optimum price point to sell. Then add photos

2

Multipoint Inspection & HPI Checks

An engineer will visit you to conduct the multi-point inspection on your vehicle and carry out a full background history check. You will receive both reports once done

3

Promote Your Car

Your car will be advertised online to targetted audience, and you will also benefit from our TV, Radio and Print campaigns. It will also be broadcasted to the Servicefy Community

4

Accept Viewings

You are in control. You decide when viewings should be held. You can manage your availability for viewings and confirm viewing requests instantly with few clicks

5

Close Deal

Once we get confirmation of ownership exchange from you and the buyer, we will transfer your money into your account immediately. That's it! Done!
